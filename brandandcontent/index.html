Brand Positioning & Narrative Alignment

Across rebrands, scale-ups, and new product launches, I've led brand repositioning and messaging work that aligns product, sales, and marketing around a clear narrative, ensuring buyers immediately understand who the product is for, why it's different, and when to engage.

Map Content to User Journey Stage

I design structured content strategies mapped explicitly to funnel stages and buying moments: awareness content to drive qualified demand, consideration assets to build confidence and problem–solution fit, and conversion-focused sales enablement content that shortens sales cycles and improves win rates. This includes ICP-led messaging, verticalised narratives, use-case frameworks, ABM content when applicable, and enablement assets that sales teams actually use.

Across my roles, I've owned both the full funnel and individual funnel stages, sometimes defining and executing content strategy end to end, other times partnering with internal and external teams. This has given me hands-on exposure to a wide range of content formats, including thought leadership, blogs, research reports and ebooks, fact sheets, infographics, webinars, podcasts, and sales enablement assets, and a strong, practical understanding of how each performs across buying stages and where it creates the most value.

Become a Thought Leader in Your Industry

Across my roles at companies such as Wise, Spendesk, Oracle, OFX, and others, a consistent focus has been building credible thought leadership within the industries we operate in. This has meant developing educational, persona-driven content that goes beyond promotion to genuinely train and inform the audiences we want to reach.

By addressing the real challenges, responsibilities, and decision criteria of both decision makers and telling influencers, I help position the company as a trusted source of insight and guidance, not just a vendor. Over time, this approach turns content into a lever for trust, consideration, and conversion, supporting demand generation and long-term brand authority.
