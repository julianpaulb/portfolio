Interim Product Marketing & Demand Generation Director · Global · Wise Business Account & Wise Platform · Payments & Fintech
Fintech · Global

Wise is widely known for B2C low-cost international transfers. My mandate was to build and scale B2B globally across two distinct offerings: Wise Business (SMB account) and Wise Platform (payment infrastructure API for banks, fintechs, payroll providers, and spend-management platforms).

  • B2B grew from 3% to 25% of Wise’s total FX volume (2022–2025). Wise Platform generated $7M ARR in year one through verticalised inbound campaigns. Established a repeatable B2B GTM engine now used to win partnerships with leading banks, payroll providers, and fintech platforms.
  • Proud to have been actively involved in the global brand transition from TransferWise to Wise, overseeing the rollout of the new brand identity, visual system, and messaging shift across B2B marketing channels.
  • Scaled Wise B2B from 3% to 25% of total FX volume, generating $7M ARR in year one and building the repeatable GTM engine behind it.
  • Supported the global TransferWise → Wise rebrand, rolling out the new identity and messaging across B2B marketing channels.
Key Challenges
  • Wise’s strong B2C identity created friction in building B2B credibility with enterprise buyers, banks, and platform partners
  • Two fundamentally different products (SMB account vs infrastructure API) required distinct positioning, personas, and motions
  • Building global B2B GTM from near-zero across EMEA, APAC, and North America simultaneously
  • Wise Platform required a co-branding framework (“Powered by Wise”) that worked across partner brands without diluting either
  • Scaling inbound for an API product with a long, complex enterprise sales cycle
What I did to shift the needle?
  • Defined precise positioning and proof points for Wise Business vs Wise Platform — segment-specific value props for SMB, mid-market, and enterprise
  • Introduced scalable ABM programmes for priority accounts and verticals (banks, payroll, spend-management), enabling inbound sales pipeline
  • Led Wise Platform’s global brand and communications — including the “Powered by Wise Platform” co-branding framework adopted by bank and fintech partners
  • Designed B2B event strategy and presence at key fintech and banking events across EMEA, APAC, and North America
  • Grew B2B from a side business to a material growth driver: 3% → 25% of total FX volume (2022–2025)
  • Generated $7M ARR in year one for Wise Platform’s API offering through inbound, verticalised campaigns for banks and fintechs
Notable Outcomes
  • Defined precise positioning and proof points for Wise Business vs Wise Platform, establishing segment-specific value propositions for SMB, mid-market, and enterprise buyers.
  • Launched scalable ABM programmes targeting priority accounts and verticals including banks, fintechs, payroll providers, and spend-management platforms, building the first repeatable enterprise inbound pipeline.
